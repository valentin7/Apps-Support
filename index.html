Welcome to Evil-Hangman's support page.

After deciding it is too hard to beat Evil-Hangman in evil mode, you will have fun trolling your friends making them feel they are horrible at Hangman.

Evil-Hangman can be played in normal mode or evil mode. You will feel complete when you beat the evil mode.

  • Up to 26 number of guesses
  • Up to 24 word length
  • 250,000 English words
