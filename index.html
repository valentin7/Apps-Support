Welcome to Guru's and Evil Hangman's support page.

Evil-Hangman

After deciding it is too hard to beat Evil-Hangman in evil mode, you will have fun trolling your friends making them feel they are horrible at Hangman.

Evil-Hangman can be played in normal mode or evil mode. You will feel complete when you beat the evil mode.

  • Up to 26 number of guesses
  • Up to 24 word length
  • 250,000 English words
Guru

Guru is an app to amaze people around you.

Have a question? Guru has the answer.

You will first have to write a petition to him: "Guru, please answer this question", but you will be blown away by Guru’s answer to your question.

Please make sure you read and understand the initial tutorial. Should you want to see it again, slide the screen to the right to get to the main menu, tap below “Information”, and tap the sun in the new “secret” view.
